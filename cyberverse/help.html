Getting Started

8 articles
  • What is an NFT? ‚ñº
    An NFT (Non-Fungible Token) is a unique digital asset stored on the blockchain. Unlike cryptocurrencies, each NFT has distinct properties and cannot be exchanged on a one-to-one basis with another NFT.
  • How do I create an account? ‚ñº
    Simply connect your Web3 wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc.) to CyberVerse. No traditional signup required ‚Äì your wallet address serves as your account identifier.
  • Is CyberVerse safe to use? ‚ñº
    Yes! We use industry-leading security practices, regular smart contract audits, and multi-layer encryption to protect your assets. We've maintained 99.9% uptime since launch.

Wallet & Account

12 articles
  • Which wallets are supported? ‚ñº
    We support MetaMask, WalletConnect, Coinbase Wallet, Phantom, and most major Web3 wallets. You can connect via browser extension or mobile app.
  • How do I set up MetaMask? ‚ñº
    Download the MetaMask extension, create a new wallet, securely store your seed phrase, and add some ETH for gas fees. Then connect it to CyberVerse using the "Connect Wallet" button.
  • I forgot my wallet password. What should I do? ‚ñº
    You can recover your wallet using your seed phrase. Never share your seed phrase with anyone. If you've lost it, unfortunately the wallet cannot be recovered.

Buying & Selling

15 articles
  • How do I buy an NFT? ‚ñº
    Browse NFTs, click "Buy Now" on your chosen item, confirm the transaction in your wallet, and pay the gas fee. The NFT will appear in your wallet once the transaction is confirmed.
  • What are gas fees? ‚ñº
    Gas fees are transaction costs paid to blockchain validators. They vary based on network congestion. We recommend transacting during off-peak hours for lower fees.
  • Can I make an offer below the listing price? ‚ñº
    Yes! You can make offers on any listed NFT. The seller can accept, counter, or decline your offer. Offers expire after 7 days unless specified otherwise.

Creating NFTs

10 articles
  • How do I mint my first NFT? ‚ñº
    Go to Create ‚Üí Mint NFT, upload your file, fill in details (name, description, properties), set your price and royalties, then click "Mint NFT". Confirm the transaction in your wallet.
  • What file formats are supported? ‚ñº
    We support JPG, PNG, GIF, SVG, MP4, WEBM, MP3, WAV, and more. Maximum file size is 100MB. For best results, use high-resolution images.
  • How do royalties work? ‚ñº
    Set royalties (up to 50%) when minting. You'll automatically receive your percentage whenever your NFT is resold on secondary markets. Royalties are enforced on-chain.

Technical Issues

9 articles
  • My transaction is stuck pending. What do I do? ‚ñº
    Check your transaction on Etherscan. You can speed it up by increasing gas fees or wait for it to process. Most transactions complete within 15 minutes during normal network conditions.
  • Why can't I see my NFT? ‚ñº
    NFTs can take a few minutes to appear after minting. Try refreshing your browser or checking your wallet directly. If it's been over an hour, contact support.
  • The site won't load properly ‚ñº
    Clear your browser cache and cookies, try a different browser, or disable ad blockers. Make sure your wallet extension is updated to the latest version.

Security & Safety

11 articles
  • How do I protect my wallet? ‚ñº
    Never share your seed phrase or private keys. Use a hardware wallet for large amounts. Enable two-factor authentication where possible. Be cautious of phishing attempts.
  • How do I spot fake NFTs? ‚ñº
    Look for verified creator badges, check the contract address, review the creator's history, and be wary of prices that seem too good to be true.
  • What should I do if I suspect a scam? ‚ñº
    Report it immediately through our support system. Provide as much detail as possible including wallet addresses, transaction hashes, and screenshots.
