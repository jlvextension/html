We charge a 2.5% platform fee on successful sales. Minting is free, but you'll need to pay gas fees on the blockchain network.
How long does verification take? ‚ñº
Creator verification typically takes 2-5 business days. You'll need to provide social media links and portfolio samples.
Can I sell NFTs created on other platforms? ‚ñº
Yes! CyberVerse supports cross-platform trading. You can list any ERC-721 or ERC-1155 NFT from other marketplaces.
What payment methods do you accept? ‚ñº
We accept ETH, WETH, USDC, and other major cryptocurrencies. Credit card support for buying crypto is coming soon!
How do royalties work? ‚ñº
Creators can set royalties up to 50% on secondary sales. You'll automatically receive your share whenever your NFT is resold.
