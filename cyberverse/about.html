Our Story

Founded in 2022, CyberVerse NFT emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to democratize digital ownership and create a thriving ecosystem where creators and collectors can connect, trade, and thrive together.

We recognized that the NFT space was fragmented, complex, and often intimidating for newcomers. Our mission became clear ‚Äì build a platform that combines cutting-edge blockchain technology with an intuitive, cyberpunk-inspired user experience that makes NFTs accessible to everyone.

Today, CyberVerse has grown into one of the leading NFT marketplaces, hosting over 500,000 digital assets, facilitating $2.5 billion in trading volume, and supporting a vibrant community of 150,000+ active users and 25,000+ verified creators.

But we're just getting started. We're building the infrastructure for the next generation of digital culture, where ownership is transparent, creators are fairly compensated, and communities have real power.
